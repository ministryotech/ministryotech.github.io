Relaunching The Ministry

By Keith Jackson 13/05/2015

Today we officially relaunch The Ministry of Technology.

The Website

With a brand spanking cutting edge new responsive website design courtesy of Paul Jackson and a mobile first design philosophy, the new website should be able to target a wider audience than ever before. The blog pages have been enhanced significantly, with Disqus now integrated to enable feedback to the articles.

The Services

This is also the launch of our new services catalogue. The Ministry of Technology has always existed to provide you with a wide variety of services tailored to fit your specific needs. The new service catalogue has been designed to help explain exactly what kind of services we can offer.

Partners

The new partners section has been created to provide a platform for highlighting the skilled experts that we work with. Over the coming months we expect this section to grow significantly as we start incorporating more of our associate partners into the site.

For Developers

The old site was a mixed collection of information targeting both businesses and developers. With the new site design we have isolated these elements for a much improved user experience, with business and strategic information in the forefront and with a dedicated sub-site provided for developers. Currently the developers section consists largely of developer orientated blog entries. We have an extensive roadmap for enhancing this section over the next few months to incorporate links to all our open source projects and details of the mentoring services that we can provide.
